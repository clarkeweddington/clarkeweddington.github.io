Toronto Travel Ad

This was a project I did alongside the Seattle Ad project. I created the project using Photoshop. Every choice was made keeping the tone of the city in mind. I chose these cities because I thought it would be interesting to play with their color palettes and tall tourist attractions. This set the tone exactly how I wanted it.
